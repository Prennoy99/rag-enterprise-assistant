Acme Robotics — Remote Work Policy
Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Eligibility
All full-time employees who have completed their 90-day probationary period are eligible to apply for remote work arrangements. Part-time and contract employees are not eligible under this policy.

2. Work Arrangement Types
Employees may choose between three arrangements: Fully Remote, Hybrid (minimum 2 office days per week), or Fully On-site. Arrangement changes require manager approval and take effect at the start of the following month.

3. Equipment Stipend
Approved remote employees receive a one-time equipment stipend of $800 to cover a monitor, chair, and desk. A recurring $50 monthly stipend covers internet costs. Stipends are paid via expense reimbursement within 30 days of a valid receipt submission.

4. Core Hours
All employees, regardless of arrangement, must be available for core collaboration hours between 10:00 AM and 3:00 PM in their local time zone, Monday through Friday. Outside core hours, employees may set their own schedule as long as they meet their weekly 40-hour commitment.

5. Security Requirements
Remote employees must use company-issued laptops with full-disk encryption enabled. Personal devices may not be used to access internal systems. All remote work must occur over a WPA3-secured home network or an approved VPN connection.

6. International Remote Work
Employees wishing to work remotely from a country other than their country of employment must submit a request at least 60 days in advance. Approval depends on tax and legal review and is not guaranteed. International remote work is capped at 90 days per calendar year.

7. Review Period
Remote work arrangements are reviewed annually each January. Managers may revoke remote status if performance expectations are not met, with a 30-day written notice.
